How do messengers link their bank accounts?

Once you are an approved messenger, you will receive your earnings weekly via direct deposit. After passing the verification process on the mobile app, you will be asked to fill out your bank account information. You'll also need to provide your tax information.


Click on "Routing" to enter your 9 digit routing number and account number below. The easiest way to access these numbers is by referring to a check from your bank.




Please note that you will have to fill out your W-9 tax form after you insert your bank information.
