So you need to send a package?

Step One

Place a request for pickup by choosing a date, time and location. Make your messenger selection based on messenger rating.

Step Two

After selecting your messenger work out the details of passing over your goods to them through the app based chat system or phone call.

Step Three

Once the messenger arrives at your location, they will carefully inspect the package then take it to your destination.

Step Four

While en route to the destination you can track the item's location with time GPS. After the delivery is complete you will receive a confirmation request.

Step Five

Check with the receiver that they received the item(s) and respond to the confirmation request.

Step Six

Senders and messengers rate each other to preserve and improve our community's high-quality standards!
