Accepted Type of Credit Cards


What type of credit cards can I use?


Postmob works with major credit card including, American Express, JCB,Visa,Mastercard,Discover and diners club,as well as debit cards tied to a checking account. .Note that we are unable to accept prepaid credit cards, secured credit cards, or debit cards not tied to a regular checking account.

What if I keep getting error messages?


Note that we are unable to accept prepaid credit cards, secured credit cards, or debit cards not tied to a regular checking account. If you're having trouble entering your card and would like us to look into it, we'd be happy to help! Just contact us with the following information:

Name on the card (first and last)
Last 4 digits of the card number
Expiration date
Card type (credit card, debit, pre-paid, etc.)
Card issuer (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.)

What if my payment didn't go through?


Don't worry. Next time you open the app you will be asked to enter payment information, the failed payment will automatically go and you'll receive an email with the receipt.

Who processes yours credit card information?


Stripe processes our credit card transactions for rides. Please review Stripe's FAQ should you have any questions or concerns about the security of your payment information.
