Receipt Overview

Receipt Overview


Receipts are sent out immediately after you submit a rating and payment to your messenger. The recommended payment will automatically be taken off after 24 hours, and a delivery receipt will be sent to you. It will go to the email address you have on file with us.


What can I see on the receipt?


You can see the amount you were charged for the delivery, as well as the pick up location and destination.

How do I change my email to stop these receipts?


Just click on the profile icon in the mobile app, click on account settings and follow the instructions.
