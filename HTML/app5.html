Cancelling a Postmob Request


If you no longer require a delivery, feel free to cancel the request. This will notify the messenger and free them up to accept requests from other community members.

To cancel a ride on an IPhone, tap the cancel icon on the tracking screen. It will then ask you if you are sure or not.
