What is PostMob?

PostMob is a mobile application that will work as a shipping platform in order to connect
travelers with people looking to ship goods

Get a better deal!

Travelers have now the ability to earn extra cash, and satisfy the customer
by offering a better deal to shit goods! Here's how it works:

Know the price up front

PostMob will give you a fixed price as soon as you specify your destination. We work hard on giving you the best deal out there! Just tell us where you want it shipped, and we will take care of the rest!

Choose your messenger
Postmob gives you the flexibility to choose your courier experience, by choosing your traveler at the time that is convenient for you.
