Payment and Tips


How do I pay for Postmob?


Paying for Postmob deliveries is done entirely through the app, and is completely cash-free! When you log into the app for the first time, you will be asked to input your payment information. Once a delivery is complete, users will be asked to pay within the Postmob App.

If users do not use or open the app within 24 hours of the delivery being completed, the payment will automatically process.


How can I update my payment information?


First you need to go to the profile screen. After that you need to click on the payment icon. It will guide you through the steps for changing your credit card.

How can I tip?


After an awesome delivery job, you might feel generous enough to tip your messenger. It's certainly not required but tipping goes a long way in motivating new messengers in this new community.

If you want to show your appreciation for the delivery job, just select the amount you would like to tip on the payment screen. If you add enough, the messenger will transform into "The Flash".

Please note that all tips will be charged to the credit card on file, and not pulled from any existing ride credits.

Do I have to pay right away?


You have 24 hours to go back into the app, pay your messenger and give rating. If you forgot to go back within the app after 24 hours, the recommended amount will be automatically paid and no rating will be given.

How can I get a receipt for my delivery?


You will be emailed a receipt for every delivery. Each receipt shows the amount charged to your credit card, the delivery time and location, as well as your messengers name. The receipts will be sent to the email you signed up with.
