Postmob App Basics

Postmob App Basics


  1. Place a request for pickup by choosing date, time and location. Make your messenger selections based on messenger rating.
  2. After selecting your messenger work out the details of delivery through the app based chat system.
  3. Once the messenger arrives at your location, they will carefully inspect the package. After they agree that it is safe, they will deliver your package to your destination.
  4. While en route to the destination you can track the item's location with real time gps. After the delivery is complete you will receive a confirmation, additionally you can add tips and rating.

Check with the receiver that they received the item(s) and respond to the confirmation request.


Senders and messengers rate each other in order to preserve and improve our community's high-quality standards!

Then what?


Once a messenger has accepted your request, you'll be able to see their name, rating, and picture.

You'll be able to see your messenger's ETA, and watch as they approach you on the map.

If you have any special instructions, or need to speak to your driver while they are on their way, you can message them by tapping the messenger button.

You'll receive a text message when your messenger arrives!

What happens after the delivery?


Once your messenger completes the delivery, you will see the payment and rating page in the app. You have 24 hours to submit your payment and rating.
This page will still be available even if you close the app. After 24 hours, this information will be automatically be submitted. You will receive a receipt with all payment information in the email we send you.


  1. Payment Total: This is the total amount paid for your order.
  2. Rate Your Messenger: Move on to rate your experience with your messenger on a scale of 1 to 5 stars, 5 being the best.
  3. Submit Button: Once you hit the submit button, both your payment and rating will be submitted to our system. You will receive a receipt with all payment information via email.

Feedback

If you ever have a messenger that doesn't meet your expectations, we want to know about it!

If you rate your messenger 3 stars or lower, you will not be paired with them again.
