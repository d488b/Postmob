Background Check


Every messenger is screened for criminal offenses. The criminal background checks local, county, and national level databases, and also scans within the national sex offender registries.

  • No violent crimes
  • No sexual offenses
  • No theft
  • No property damage
  • No felonies
  • No drug-related offenses

Two-Way Rating


You are in control. Messengers and senders rate each other after each delivery. If you rate someone 3 stars or less, you'll never be matched with them again. If a messenger's average falls below 3.6 out of 5 stars, they are no longer able to be a Postmob messenger. This way we assure that the messengers are up to high quality standards.
