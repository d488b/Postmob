About Us

Postmob is bringing the social experience to delivery

Postmob is a mobile application that will work as a shipping platform in order to connect travelers with people looking for a delivery job. We are different from other services out there, by giving the customer the flexibility to ship their goods. Community members will be able to agree on a price and time of job. Postmob wants to create a network, where people who are traveling can earn extra cash by taking advantage of their route and delivering products that the user wants to send.
